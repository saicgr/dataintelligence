Privacy Policy

ByteShards is built offline-first. The short version: your study data lives on your device, an account is optional, we don't sell data, and we don't show ads.

What we collect

  • No account (default): your progress — streaks, XP, reviewed cards, saved cards, settings — is stored only on your device. We can't see it.
  • If you sign in (optional): we store your email or Apple ID identifier and your synced study progress with our backend provider (Supabase) so leagues, friends, and cross-device sync work.
  • Usage analytics: anonymous app events (e.g. "session completed") via PostHog to understand which features matter. No card answers, no personal content.
  • Purchases: handled entirely by Apple App Store / Google Play. We never see your payment details — only a confirmation that Pro is active.

What we don't do

  • No selling or renting of personal data. No third-party advertising or ad SDKs.
  • No AI processing of your data at runtime — the code judge runs entirely on your device.
  • No collection from children: ByteShards is intended for users 13 and older.

Sharing & processors

We use a small set of processors strictly to run the service: Supabase (accounts & sync), PostHog (anonymous analytics), Apple/Google (distribution & billing), and Expo/EAS (app updates). Each receives only what it needs.

Retention & your rights

Synced data is kept while your account exists and deleted within 30 days of account deletion (see Delete your account). You may request a copy or correction of your data at support@byteshards.dev. Local data is removed by uninstalling the app.

Terms of Service

The service

ByteShards is a study app for technical interview preparation and skills upkeep. The free core (all tracks, answers, daily sessions with limits) is available without payment. ByteShards Pro unlocks the weekly fresh stream, incident debugging, mock & voice interviews, the JD analyzer, and unlimited sessions, code runs and custom decks.

Subscriptions & purchases

  • Pro is offered as a monthly or yearly auto-renewing subscription, or a one-time lifetime purchase, billed through your Apple or Google account.

  • Cancelling Pro never removes the free core.

Delete your account

You can delete your ByteShards account and all synced data at any time. Two ways:

1 · In the app (fastest)

Profile → Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, synced progress, league history, and friends links. The action is immediate and cannot be undone.

2 · By email

Email support@byteshards.dev with the subject "Delete my account" from the address you signed in with. We verify and delete within 30 days, and confirm when done.

What gets deleted — and what doesn't

  • Deleted: account identifiers, synced study progress, league/contest history, friend connections, and analytics linked to your account.
  • Not affected: data stored only on your device (uninstall the app to remove it), and purchase records held by Apple/Google — those are managed in your store account.
  • Pro purchases: deleting your account does not cancel a subscription — cancel it in App Store / Google Play settings. Lifetime purchases can be restored later via "Restore purchases" on the same store account.
